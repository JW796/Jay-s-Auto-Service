Offering a Wide Range of Automotive Repair and Maintenance Services

Jay's is your one-stop shop for all your auto servicing and repair needs. We're equipped to work on all makes and models of domestic and foreign cars. You name it, we service it! Complete customer satisfaction is guaranteed. Jay's Auto Services takes care of everything when it comes to servicing and maintaining your vehicle. Our services include but are not limited to:


2 & 4 wheel alignment service. Tire sales & repairs. Wheel balancing. Shocks, struts, ball joints, tie rods.




Electrical sytem diagnostics, batteries, alternators, starters, wiring, computer diagnostics.





Complete heating, air conditioning and cooling system service. Compressors, heater cores, A/C charging, water pumps.



Under hood services. Maintenance, oil & lube services, tune-ups, timing belts, belt & hoses.





Complete brake service including brake pads, brake shoes, rotors, calipers, brake hoses, master cylinders.
